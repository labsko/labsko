Light becomes data

Imagine a world where every light connects us with unprecedented data and bandwidth.

Imagine LiFi...

LiFi uses light to send wireless data embedded in its beam.

A LiFi enabled device converts the beam of light into an electrical signal.

The signal is then converted back into data.

The device sends back data using invisible light.

LiFi is high speed bi-directional and fully networked light communications.

We are ready to bring LiFi to the world. Are you?
